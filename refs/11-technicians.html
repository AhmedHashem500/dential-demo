The payroll equation
6,000 base − 1,000 advances − 200 deductions = EGP 4,800 net
Salary is never accrued — the month crystallizes at the run (§10.9). Amber = advances (cash already out), red = deductions (no cash ever).
Advance vs deduction — the cash difference
Advance (سلفة) = cash-out NOW via Money + reduces net
Deduction = net reduction only, no cash, reason audited
Both close at the payroll run. Advances taken after a run roll to the next month.
Payroll month — status pair
⏲ Due at run✓ Paid
Paid months show their Money voucher refs — every salary is a normal PV on the drawer, Paid-to = the technician.

Technicians are assignable to case_sessions (§10.4/§10.9) — the Work tab lists their sessions; feeds dashboard sessions-due.
Commission stays deferred (comp_ columns reserved in schema) — payroll is salary-only in v1.
