E · Interaction & component states
Order lifecycle — the money is at the end
Draft→Sent→Received↺ Redo
Draft = editable/deletable · Sent = out the door, cancellable w/ reason · Received = cost entered, posted, locked. Nothing financial exists before receive.
Redo — warranty, zero cost
↺ Redo posts NOTHING — the lab re-does its own work for free; the row stays in the ledger as EGP 0 for visibility.
Every redo is counted per lab (and per case when linked) — the §10.10-F quality report ranks labs by redo rate. The flag lives on the receive dialog.
Case link — optional chip
⛭ 0231 · Mariam A.— standalone
Linked orders answer “whose crown is at the lab?” and deep-link to the case; standalone stays possible (batch work, spares). The link never moves money — the case’s sale price and the lab’s cost are unrelated numbers.
Internal cost — never billed onward
Lab cost EGP 1,100 (internal)  vs  case price EGP 2,400 (doctor pays)
Outsourcing cost feeds profitability reports only (§10.8/§10.10) — no doctor-facing surface ever shows it.
Paying a lab — the Money seam
No pay button posts here. Record payment deep-links Money → Pay with CrownArt pre-filled as Paid to — lump-sum on the account.
PV-0063 in the frozen Money ledger is exactly this seam. The account statement (statement-V2 pattern) is the owed-vs-paid truth.
Deactivate — blocked while owed
Balance ≠ 0 → blocked, route to Money → Pay (same guard as suppliers/doctors). History never deleted.
Also blocked while orders are still out (Sent) — bring the work home or cancel it first.
