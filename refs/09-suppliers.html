SUP-0003 · owed EGP 6,400

E · Interaction & component states
Supplier balance — three states
Owed to supplier EGP 6,400
Settled EGP 0
Credit with supplier −EGP 500
Mirror of the doctor balance, opposite direction: positive = the lab owes. Plain words (rule 7) — “Owed to supplier”, never “payable”.
Purchase — two statuses only
ActiveCancelled
No draft: a purchase exists only once saved (atomic post). Cancel = compensating reversal of stock + payable; consumed-batch shortfalls are flagged, never silent.
Expiry on the line — Amendment D
⏲ 15 Jan 2027no expiry — not tracked for this material
Amber-bordered required field when the material’s has_expiry is ON — each line creates its batch; FEFO takes over from there. Materials without the flag never ask.
Cost vs selling price — never meet
Unit cost EGP 240 (here, internal)  ·  Fixed selling price EGP 1,500 (Materials §10.3)
Costs never appear on anything a doctor sees; margin analysis lives in Reports (§10.10 D). Changing either never rewrites history.
Paying a supplier — the Money seam
No pay button posts here. Record payment deep-links Money → Pay with NovaDent pre-filled as Paid to — lump-sum on the account, never tied to a purchase.
Same architecture as doctors: documents on one side, the single drawer on the other (§10.6). The account statement is the owed-vs-paid truth.
Deactivate — blocked while owed
Balance ≠ 0 → blocked with a route to the fix (Open Money — Pay). Settled suppliers deactivate freely; history is never deleted.
The §10.7 rule made visible — identical pattern to the doctors guard (DOC-13).
