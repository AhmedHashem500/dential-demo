Product: Dential Desktop · Module 12 — Reports · spec §10.10 + §12 [LOCKED] · v1.0.0 (unattended, recommendations applied). Read-only engine over the append-only ledgers — computed on demand, reconciles by construction, drill-down to source. Catalog = six group cards (A receivables/parties · B cash/expenses · C sales/cases [revenue-focused, no COGS] · D inventory/purchasing [internal costs + Amdt-D expiring batches] · E technicians · F quality/redo-rate) — role-gated at render. Multi-device: unsynced segments flagged on the header. v1 scope: parametrized reports + charts + the dashboard KPI front-doors; presets/scheduling/custom-builder deferred (§12).

Same render-gating rule as everywhere. Dashboard KPI tiles are these reports’ front doors.

PDFExcelCSV

Reports aggregate across device segments by UUID; unsynced devices are flagged on the report header, never silently missing (§12).
